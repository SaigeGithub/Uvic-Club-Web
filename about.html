FAQ for Uvic clubs app

reported by Saige

Q1:Why do we develop this app?
A1: As you can see,there are so many great clubs in our university,but it is kind of hard for students to find club imformation or when and where the club meet and the upcoming events. So we wanna to create a app that gather everything we can find about the clubs in Uvic,and allow students to check,even rate,like and add comments to the clubs.Also we wanna the organizers of the clubs to check the comments in order to improve their policy and updated their latest imformation.It's both useful for students and clubs

Q2:How did your group create it?
A2:Well, first we set up a time to meet to hang out in the campus,then we bring our labtops see together, share code and design, figure out difficulties .Daria is the one who come up with the idea so she have a lot of ideas of how to do it,and Priyanka is second year so she knew things about code we can come to her anytime.Christy and I work hard and learn fast, we search on google to solve tricky problems. We are a united team and help each other all the time

Q3:what part of app is not done yet?
A3:First of all, we haven't collect all the imformation about Uvic clubs.Anyway,I want the users able to add comments on our webpage and I dont know how. Also,the log in and sign up is not ready yet.And the specific information of every club should have a independent page. My app needs more javascript function. And the web sample I design is kind of more like a webpage, I might change the outlook a little bit(Size of the page, bigger word )to make it more suitable for a phone. Generally, I have to figure out how to store imformation into the server and interact with users more and work on the javascript more..
